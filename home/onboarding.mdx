# Getting Started with Embedly

Welcome to Embedly, your gateway to API Banking. Our platform is designed to seamlessly integrate financial services into your business, helping you deliver better experiences to your users. This guide will walk you through the onboarding process, so you can get started quickly and efficiently.

<Info>
	The Embedly onboarding process involves merchant profile creation, filling out
	the registration form, agreeing to terms, account activation, and setting up
	your password. Follow the steps below to get started.
</Info>

---

## Onboarding Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Embedly Account">
    To begin, you'll need to create an Embedly account.

    [![Start With Embedly](https://img.shields.io/badge/Start_with_Embedly-Get_Started-green?style=for-the-badge)](https://merchant-waas-dashboard.embedly.ng/register)

    This will take you to the Embedly registration page where you can enter your details. Please ensure all provided information is accurate to avoid any delays.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the Registration Form">
    Once on the registration page, fill out the required fields:
    ![Account Registration Form](/home/images/AccountRegistration.svg)
    <Steps>
      <Step title="First Name and Last Name">
        Use alphabetic characters only, with an optional hyphen for double-barrelled names (e.g., "John-Paul").
      </Step>

      <Step title="Email Address">
        Enter a valid email address that you can easily access for future communications.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Phone Number">
        Enter local phone number with the initial "0."
        Example: `080234567891`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Set Your Password">
        Follow the guidelines provided to create a secure password. Make sure it’s something memorable yet strong.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Agree to the Terms">
    Review our Terms of Use carefully. Once ready, check the box to provide your consent.

    <Info>
    Consenting to the terms ensures that you are aware of the rules governing the use of the platform and helps protect both your business and Embedly.
    </Info>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Your Account">
    After submitting your registration, you’ll receive an activation email. Here’s what to do next:

    - **Check Your Inbox:**
      Look for an email from Embedly with a One-Time password (OTP). If it doesn’t arrive within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.

    - **Verify Your Profile:**
      Copy the OTP and paste to verify your email address and activate your Embedly account.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Considerations

<Note>
	Double-check all information during registration. Accurate details ensure a
	smooth onboarding process and reduce the likelihood of issues down the line.
</Note>

<Tip>
	Use a strong password and avoid sharing your login credentials. Security is
	paramount, and protecting your account starts with you.
</Tip>

<Info>
	The activation email is crucial for accessing your account. If you don’t see
	it, check your spam folder or request a new one.
</Info>

---

## Key Terms to Know

- **API Key:**  
  A unique identifier that allows you to authenticate and interact with Embedly’s APIs. You’ll generate this key from your Embedly Dashboard after logging in.

- **Embedly Dashboard:**  
  Your central hub for managing your Embedly account, viewing analytics, and configuring API settings.

- **Activation Link:**  
  A link sent to your registered email address to verify your account and activate your access to Embedly.
