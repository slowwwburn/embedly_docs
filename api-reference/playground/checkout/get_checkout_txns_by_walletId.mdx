title: "Get checkout wallet transactions"
api: "GET https://checkout-staging.embedly.ng/api/v1/checkout-wallet/{walletId}/transactions"
description: "Endpoint for getting all checkout wallets under an organization using the Organization Id"
---

<ParamField path="walletId" type="string" required>
  The unique idenitifier of the checkout wallet. Exmaple: `3fa85f64-5717-4562-b3fc-2c963f66afa6`
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="organizationId" default="1" type="string">
  The unique idenitifier for your organization on Embedly. **Example:** `3fa85f64-5717-4562-b3fc-2c963f66afa6`
</ParamField>
