---
title: "Customer KYC Tier 2 Upgrade-bvn"
api: "POST customers/kyc/premium-kyc?verify=1"
description: "This endpoint enables you to upgrade a customer's KYC to Tier 2 using bvn"
---

<ParamField body="customerId" type="string" required>
	The unique identifier of the customer whose KYC tier is being upgraded.
	**Example:** `3fa85f64-5717-4562-b3fc-2c963f66afa6`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="bvn" type="string" required>
	The unique value of the customer's bvn. **Example:** `12345678909`
</ParamField>
